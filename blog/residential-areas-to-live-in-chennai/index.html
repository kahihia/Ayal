Best Places (Residential Areas) to Live in Chennai

October 20th, 2023

Explore some of the best residential areas in Chennai, highlighting their unique features & amenities, and why they might be the ideal place to call home. From beachfront neighbourhoods to bustling urban hubs and serene suburban areas, Chennai has it all.

The vibrant and diverse capital of Tamil Nadu is a city of many facets. Chennai is not just a bustling metropolis, but also a city that offers a wide range of residential options. Whether you are a professional seeking the perfect work-life balance, a family looking for a safe & nurturing environment, or a retiree searching for a peaceful abode, Chennai has something to offer everyone.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a Residential Area

When choosing a residential area in Chennai, there are several critical factors to consider, ensuring the locale aligns with your lifestyle and needs.

  1. Budget Consideration: Your budget significantly determines the type of residential area you can finalise. Ensure that your chosen area’s cost of living is within or slightly above your budget.
  2. Proximity to Work and Education: Commuting can significantly affect daily life. Look for a residential area that minimises travel time to your workplace and is close to quality educational & healthcare institutions.
  3. Safety: Research the area’s crime rates and reputation for security, to ensure you and your family feel safe & comfortable.
  4. Access to Public Transport: Easy access to metro lines, cabs, bus routes, etc. can make daily routines more convenient. Check if the area has reliable bus or train services.
  5. Essential Amenities: Consider the availability of grocery stores, clinics, pharmacies, and recreational facilities in the neighbourhood. A well-equipped locality can simplify your daily errands.
  6. Quality of Life: This includes factors like green spaces, recreational options, and the sense of community. A high quality of life can contribute to your well-being and happiness.

Top 10 Residential Areas in Chennai

  1. Velachery

    Among the most thriving residential areas to invest in Chennai, Velachery has gained popularity for several compelling reasons.

    Strategic Location and Accessibility: Situated in the southern part of Chennai, it enjoys excellent connectivity to key areas in the city. It is also close to the IT Corridor (OMR) and the Chennai International Airport, further enhancing its accessibility.

    Commercial and Educational Hubs: The presence of numerous IT companies, corporate offices, and retail centres has transformed Velachery into a bustling economic hub. Additionally, the area is well-known for hosting esteemed educational institutions, including schools, colleges, and coaching centres, making it convenient for families with students.

    Real Estate Market Trends: The real estate market in Velachery has witnessed steady growth over the years. High demand for residential properties in this area makes it one of the best places to invest in Chennai.

  2. Adyar

    Strategic location, proximity to commercial & educational hubs, and real estate market trends make Adyar a favoured residential area in Chennai, offering a blend of modernity & natural beauty by the sea.

    Location & Accessibility: Adyar is a prominent residential area in southern Chennai along the scenic shores of the Bay of Bengal. Its prime location makes it highly accessible, as it is well-connected to other parts of the city through major roads and public transport options.

    Lifestyle & Amenities: Adyar is home to several commercial & educational hubs, making it a desirable place to live. Various business centres, including IT parks and corporate offices, are located in the vicinity. The presence of prestigious academic institutions like IIT Madras and Anna University adds to its appeal, making it a highly sought-after location for students and professionals.

    Real Estate Trends: While Adyar has witnessed consistent demand in the real estate market, prices may vary significantly based on proximity to the beach, amenities, and property size. Over the years, property values have shown steady appreciation, making this locale an attractive investment option.

  3. Besant Nagar

    Besant Nagar offers a unique lifestyle, combining the tranquillity of coastal living with educational & healthcare facilities, and a vibrant cultural & recreational scene.

    Coastal Living and Scenic Views: Besant Nagar boasts of a beautiful seaside location, with the stunning Elliot’s Beach stretching along its western edge. Residents can enjoy breathtaking sunsets, leisurely walks along the sandy shores, and a refreshing sea breeze.

    Educational and Healthcare Facilities: For families with children, Besant Nagar is home to various educational institutions, including schools & colleges, making it convenient for students to access quality education. Leading healthcare facilities are located nearby, ensuring that prompt medical care is available.

    Cultural and Recreational Attractions: Besant Nagar is renowned for its cultural and recreational attractions. The Theosophical Society, known for lush greenery and serene ambience, is a cultural gem. Besant Nagar Beach is popular for picnics, beach sports, and cultural events. A multitude of restaurants, cafes, and shops offer diverse culinary experiences and shopping opportunities.

  4. Vengaivasal

    A prominent suburban area, Vengaivasal has been steadily gaining popularity as one of the best residential areas in Chennai.

    Location and Accessibility: The locale is strategically located in the southern part of Chennai, making it accessible to various parts of the city. It is near key roadways such as Velachery-Tambaram Road and GST Road, making it convenient for commuters.

    Lifestyle and Amenities: Vengaivasal offers a peaceful, laidback lifestyle compared to the bustling city centre. The area is known for its proximity to educational institutions, including schools and engineering colleges. While nearby locales provide ample options for shopping & entertainment, parks and green spaces have enhanced the quality of life in the area.

    Real Estate Trends: The real estate market in Vengaivasal has shown steady growth in recent years. As more families and professionals seek housing options away from the city’s congestion, this area has become more desirable. The market includes a mix of independent houses, apartments, and gated communities.

  5. Porur

    A western suburb of Chennai, Porur has been gaining recognition as an area that offers excellent housing options while ensuring excellent connectivity.

    Diverse Housing Options & Good Connectivity: Porur provides many housing options, including apartments, independent houses, and gated communities. The area’s excellent connectivity is a significant advantage. It is located near key roads, including Chennai Bypass Road and Mount Poonamallee Road, making it easy to access other parts of the city.

    Proximity to Industrial Zones: Porur’s strategic location near many industrial areas and IT parks makes it a compelling proposition to invest in. The Sriperumbudur Industrial Corridor, known for housing numerous manufacturing companies, is not far away.

    Educational and Healthcare Facilities: The locality offers access to various educational institutions, including schools, colleges, and coaching centres. Porur is also home to healthcare facilities, clinics, and hospitals, ensuring residents can access medical services when needed.

  6. Poonamallee

    Strategic location, peaceful lifestyle, and excellent amenities make Poonamallee one of the best places to live in Chennai. Homebuyers looking to balance urban connectivity with suburban charm can consider this area.

    Location and Accessibility: Located on the Chennai-Bangalore highway, this area is a bit away from the city centre – making it an attractive option for those seeking a more peaceful residential environment while still having access to the city. Poonamallee is served by regular bus services and other public transport options, ensuring smooth connectivity to key areas of the city.

    Lifestyle and Amenities: The locale offers a comfortable and serene lifestyle away from the hustle & bustle of the city. Poonamallee is known for its greenery and open spaces, making it an attractive choice for families and nature enthusiasts.

    Real Estate Trends: The real estate market in Poonamallee has been steadily growing in recent years. With its proximity to vital industrial zones and IT parks, the demand for housing in this area has increased. Residential properties in Poonamallee range from budget-friendly apartments to spacious villas & plots.

  7. Anna Nagar

    A prominent residential area, Anna Nagar is known for its excellent location, seamless connectivity, quality education & healthcare facilities, and diverse residential options.

    Central Location and Connectivity: Anna Nagar is close to the city centre, making it convenient for residents to access various parts and important offices in Chennai. The area boasts of excellent connectivity through road networks, with multiple bus routes and major arterial roads.

    Education & Healthcare: Anna Nagar is home to leading educational institutions, both schools and colleges. Additionally, the area has numerous hospitals & clinics, providing residents with access to medical services and healthcare facilities.

    Residential Options and Property Trends: Anna Nagar offers diverse residential options. You can find everything from independent houses to apartments and gated communities. Property trends in Anna Nagar have shown a steadily growing demand, making it a popular choice for homebuyers & investors.

  8. ECR (East Coast Road)

    A picturesque stretch in Chennai, ECR (East Coast Road) offers beachside living, tranquillity, high-end residential options, and a wealth of leisure & recreation opportunities.

    Beachside Living and Tranquillity: One of the most captivating aspects of ECR is its proximity to the Bay of Bengal. Residents along this coastal stretch enjoy the luxury of beachside living. It is an ideal choice for those seeking a peaceful and serene residential experience.

    High-end Residential Options: ECR is synonymous with luxury and high-end living. The area boasts of an array of upscale residential options, including beachfront villas, luxury apartments, and gated communities.

    Leisure and Recreation Opportunities: The road is dotted with beach resorts, restaurants, and cafes where you can relish delicious cuisine and enjoy the beach ambience. For nature enthusiasts, ECR beach and lush greenery provide many opportunities for exploration & relaxation.

Upcoming Residential Areas

  1. Pallikaranai

    A neighbourhood in the southern part of Chennai, Pallikaranai is characterised by proximity to IT parks, affordable housing options, and future potential growth.

    Budget Housing Options: Pallikaranai is an attractive choice for individuals and families seeking residential solutions that are not heavy on the wallet.

    Proximity to IT Parks: The strategic location of Pallikaranai places it within reasonable commuting distance of various IT parks and employment hubs in Chennai. Professionals working in the IT sector often find this area convenient due to its proximity to companies and tech parks in the southern part of the city.

    Future Growth Potential: Pallikaranai has developed over the years, and is now poised for future growth. Ongoing infrastructure improvements and current affordability make it a promising area for real estate investment.

  2. Perumbakkam

    Situated amidst the southern suburbs of Chennai, Perumbakkam is an emerging residential hub known for its affordable and mid-range housing options, connectivity, and essential amenities.

    Emerging Residential Hub: Perumbakkam is ideal for individuals and families looking for budget housing options. The area offers apartments, gated communities, and independent houses catering to various budgets.

    Affordable and Mid-range Housing: Perumbakkam has witnessed significant development over the years, transforming it into an emerging residential hub. The real estate market in this area has been on the rise, and it has become an attractive choice for both homebuyers and property investors.

    Connectivity and Amenities: Perumbakkam benefits from good connectivity to various parts of Chennai. It is well-connected by road networks and public transport, making daily commutes convenient. Additionally, the area has essential amenities such as grocery stores, schools, healthcare facilities, and recreational spaces, ensuring that residents can access all the necessities that are required for a comfortable lifestyle.

Final Thoughts

Whether you are seeking the tranquillity of beachside living on ECR, the central convenience of Anna Nagar, or the affordability of Perumbakkam, Chennai caters to a diverse range of requirements. Selecting the ideal residential area is a deeply personal decision – influenced by factors like proximity to work & education, safety, quality of life, essential amenities, budget, and public transport. Prioritising your specific needs and preferences is necessary while taking this crucial decision.

With its rich heritage, modern amenities, and diverse neighbourhoods, Chennai truly has something to offer everyone. As you embark on the journey to find the perfect place to call home, Chennai’s warm hospitality and diverse living spaces will ensure that you not only find a house but a home where your dreams and aspirations can flourish.

FAQs

1. Which area is best for residential in Chennai?

The best residential area in Chennai includes Velachery for its central location and amenities, ECR for beachside living, and OMR for its IT corridor. The ideal place for you will depend on your budget, proximity to work and education, and lifestyle preferences.

2. Which is the safest area in Chennai?

Safest areas in Chennai include Adyar, Mylapore, Velachery, and Besant Nagar. However, safety can vary even within these areas, so it is advisable to research specific localities and consider recent crime statistics.

3. Which is the cleanest area in Chennai?

Cleanest areas in Chennai are Alwarpet, Adyar, and Boat Club Road. The Chennai Corporation works continuously to maintain cleanliness throughout the city.

4. Which is the most developing area in Chennai?

Most developing area in Chennai is OMR (Old Mahabalipuram Road), due to its growth as an IT and business corridor. It has seen significant infrastructural and real estate development in recent years.

5. Which is the more developed city, Chennai or Bangalore?

Chennai and Bangalore are both well-developed, major cities in South India. Bangalore is often considered India's tech & startup hub, while Chennai is known for its diverse industrial base and cultural heritage. The development in these cities depends on various parameters, and it is challenging to make a definitive comparison.

6. Which areas are fast developing near Chennai?

Areas fast developing near Chennai are OMR (Old Mahabalipuram Road), Perumbakkam, Sriperumbudur, Oragadam, and GST Road.
