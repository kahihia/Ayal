Growth and Development Factors

Sector 106 in Gurgaon has experienced rapid growth and development driven by numerous factors – including economic development, infrastructure improvement, and availability of social amenities. Here is a breakdown of the critical growth and development factors in the area:

Economic Development

  • Business & Commercial Hubs: Sector 106 benefits from its proximity to established business and commercial hubs in Gurgaon, such as Cyber City, Cyber Hub, and Golf Course Extension Road. These host a plethora of multinational corporations, IT companies, startups, and commercial establishments – contributing to the area’s economic vibrancy.

Infrastructure Development

  • Road Connectivity: The locale enjoys excellent road connectivity, mainly due to its proximity to Dwarka Expressway (Northern Peripheral Road) and Delhi-Jaipur Highway (NH48). These arterial roads provide seamless connectivity to key destinations in Delhi-NCR – including Indira Gandhi International Airport, South Delhi, West Delhi, Gurgaon, and Jaipur.
  • Public Transport Facilities: The area is well-served by public transport facilities, including buses, auto-rickshaws, and cabs. Additionally, the proposed metro connectivity through the Dwarka Expressway corridor will further enhance the public transport infrastructure in Sector 106, improving accessibility for homebuyers and commuters.

Social Amenities

  • Educational Institutions: Sector 106 boasts a range of reputed educational institutions, including schools, colleges, and coaching centres, catering to the academic needs of students across different age groups.
  • Healthcare Facilities: The area is dotted with healthcare facilities, including hospitals, clinics, and diagnostic centres that offer comprehensive medical services and emergency care to homebuyers & residents.
  • Shopping & Entertainment Centres: Being a rapidly growing area of Gurgaon, Sector 106 has a plethora of shopping and entertainment options – with malls, multiplexes, retail outlets, and recreational centres located in the vicinity.
