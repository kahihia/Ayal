Best Places (Residential Areas) to Live in Trivandrum

October 13th, 2023

Trivandrum’s top residential areas offer diverse living experiences. From the elegance of Kowdiar to the serene shores of Akkulam, the convenience of Pattom, the emerging appeal of Kazhakkottam, and the tranquility of Mangalapuram, there’s a place for everyone in this sprawling metropolis.

Trivandrum, the enchanting capital city of Kerala, beckons with its perfect blend of unique tradition and modernity. It is an enticing choice for those seeking an ideal residential destination in the southern coastal paradise of India. Nestled amidst this captivating urban tapestry is Akkulam, a prime residential area that stands out as one of the city’s top places to live. Akkulam boasts of a picturesque lakeside setting, providing residents with stunning water views and a serene ambience.

Its strategic location – near key educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and commercial hubs – makes it a favoured choice for families and professionals alike. But the allure of Trivandrum doesn’t end here. From the vibrant & bustling city centre of Thampanoor to the verdant, upscale locality of Vellayambalam, we will journey through the diverse neighbourhoods that collectively make Trivandrum an inviting and highly sought-after residential destination.

5 Best Residential Areas in Trivandrum

With a growing economy and the presence of IT companies, Trivandrum has become a magnet for professionals seeking a dynamic yet serene living environment. This vibrant city is known not only for its picturesque landscapes and cultural heritage, but also for prestigious institutions like the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST), Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), and the Rajiv Gandhi Centre for Biotechnology (RGCB).

These esteemed knowledge and research centres contribute to the city’s intellectual vibrancy, and further enhance its appeal as a hub of educational & career opportunities. As we delve deeper into Trivandrum’s residential landscape, we’ll discover the city’s diverse neighbourhoods, each offering its own unique charm & amenities.

From serene lakeside living in Akkulam to the bustling city centre of Thampanoor and the upscale Vellayambalam, Trivandrum presents many choices for those seeking their ideal home in this captivating coastal paradise. Join us on this exploration of the top residential areas that make Trivandrum an alluring place to call home.

  1. Kowdiar

    Kowdiar stands as a distinguished enclave within Trivandrum, coveted for its illustrious heritage and close ties to the esteemed Kowdiar Palace. Once the residence of the Royal Family of Travancore, the palace was commissioned by Sree Chithira Thirunal Balarama Varma. This neighbourhood exudes an air of prestige, housing celebrities, bureaucrats, and affluent entrepreneurs who relish its charm.Kowdiar boasts of accessibility to major landmarks and amenities, making it an unparalleled choice for discerning residents. Well-maintained roads, consistent power & water supply, and efficient waste management ensure a life of comfort and convenience. Nestled amidst lush greenery, parks, and open spaces, Kowdiar offers a cosmopolitan lifestyle in a serene environment. The backdrop of nature’s beauty complements the peaceful living experience.For enthusiasts of the finer things in life, the Trivandrum Golf Club presents an exclusive opportunity to indulge in the sport. If you seek a residence in the heart of the city, that seamlessly blends heritage with modernity, Kowdiar beckons as the epitome of a majestic lifestyle.

  2. Akkulam

    Nestled in the tranquil suburbs of Trivandrum, Akkulam offers an escape from the city’s chaos. This residential haven enjoys excellent connectivity, particularly to Technopark, one of India’s largest IT parks, making it an ideal residence for tech professionals seeking both convenience and tranquility. Additionally, Akkulam has proximity to significant landmarks such as the Southern Air Command, Air Force Museum, and the scenic Akkulam Tourist Village.

    Educational Institutions:

    Loyola School Trivandrum, School of the Good Shepherd, National Institute of Speech and Hearing (NISH), and College of Engineering (CET) Trivandrum are in close proximity.

    Healthcare:

    For healthcare needs, the esteemed KIMSHEALTH Hospital, known for its exceptional medical care and state-of-the-art facilities, is conveniently located nearby.

    Public transport networks seamlessly connect Akkulam to key areas in Trivandrum, ensuring easy access to the city’s amenities. For those seeking their dream home in this idyllic locale, SOBHA Meadows – Whispering Hill, a luxury apartment project, promises the charming side of life amidst the beauty & convenience of Akkulam.

  3. Pattom

    Not far from the bustling city centre, Pattom offers residents the perfect blend of urban convenience and serene ambiance. This locale provides easy access to iconic landmarks and several healthcare facilities. Pattom boasts of a diverse range of housing options, catering to various preferences. From upscale apartments to spacious villas, this area offers a multitude of lifestyle choices.

    Landmarks:

    Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Napier Museum, and the picturesque Kowdiar Palace.

    Schools & Education Institutions:

    Kendriya Vidyalaya Pattom, Loyola School, Christ Nagar School, Kerala Institute of Medical Sciences (KIMS)

    With its ideal blend of location, housing choices, and essential amenities, Pattom is a prime residential area in Trivandrum, appealing to many residents looking for an urban yet peaceful lifestyle.

  4. Kazhakkoottam

    Kazhakkoottam has emerged as one of the most vibrant and sought-after neighbourhoods in the city. Over the past decade, it has witnessed a remarkable transformation, with a population of over 60,000 individuals employed at Technopark, Kerala’s pioneering IT park.It has direct access to NH47 and the Main Central Road, offering a hassle-free commute that bypasses traffic at the city centre. Beyond the excellent connectivity, the area boasts of proximity to several key attractions, such as Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre and the Trivandrum International Stadium, famously known as the Greenfield Stadium.

    Often referred to as Kerala’s IT capital, the area continues to experience robust real estate growth, propelled by its proximity to employment hubs like Technopark, well-maintained municipal amenities, and efficient road and rail networks for those seeking a dynamic and well-connected living environment.

  5. Mangalapuram

    Mangalapuram emerges as an excellent residential enclave, offering a life cocooned in lush greenery, far removed from the urban hustle and bustle. This idyllic neighbourhood provides a serene and harmonious living environment. It enjoys seamless connectivity via the Outer Ring Road, creating a convenient link between Vizhinjam Seaport and this peaceful retreat.Surrounded by verdant landscapes, residents here revel in the proximity to the enchanting Kadinamkulam Lake, the Bio 360 Life Sciences Park, Vellanikkal Paramukal Viewpoint, and the Kumaranasan Memorial.

    Mangalapuram enjoys easy access to the vibrant Technocity and houses several prominent institutions and schools like Trivandrum International School and the Sree Gokulam Medical College & Research Foundation.

Factors to Consider When Choosing a Residential Area in Trivandrum

When embarking on the journey to find the best areas to invest in Trivandrum, do consider key factors that play a vital role in giving the best long-term living experience.

  1. Budget Considerations:

    Your budget is the first and foremost factor to consider. Trivandrum offers a diverse range of residential areas, each with its own pricing trends. Determine how much you are willing to invest in your dream home, and explore neighbourhoods that align with that estimate.

  2. Proximity to Workplace and Transportation:

    Commute times can significantly impact your daily life. Consider the proximity of your potential residential area to your workplace. Trivandrum’s public transportation network, including buses and the upcoming metro system, can further ease your daily commute. Choosing a location with excellent connectivity to key areas can save you valuable time.

  3. Quality of Schools and Healthcare Facilities:

    For families, the quality of schools in the area is of paramount importance. Research the availability and reputation of schools in the neighbourhood. Additionally, access to healthcare facilities should be a consideration. Ensure that you have easy and reliable medical services nearby.

  4. Safety and Security:

    Research the area’s crime rates and general safety reputation. Well-lit streets, vigilant security measures, and a peaceful community atmosphere can create a secure living environment for your family.

  5. Future Growth Prospects:

    While focusing on the present is essential, considering the future is equally vital. Assess the growth prospects of the residential area. Factors like upcoming infrastructure projects, commercial developments, and property value trends can provide valuable insights into the area’s long-term potential.

Real Estate Trends in Trivandrum

Being well-versed in the trends & factors that sway property prices in a specific locality equips buyers to assess the fairness of a property’s cost. Moreover, it aids in identifying potential opportunities and crafting strategic plans. In essence, market comprehension is indispensable for buyers, serving as a shield against risks, ensuring prudent decision-making, and securing utmost value for their investment.

Factors Influencing the Real Estate Market in Trivandrum

  1. Government Regulations and Policies:

    These wield significant influence over the real estate market in Trivandrum, much like in the rest of India. Alterations in tax laws, zoning regulations, and property-related legislation can affect the equilibrium of demand & supply in a specific area.

  2. Infrastructure Development:

    The growth & enhancement of infrastructure, including roads, airports, and public transportation systems, play a pivotal role in determining the real estate landscape’s worth. Well-developed infrastructure can bolster property values, making accessibility & connectivity critical drivers in real estate demand.

  3. Economic Factors:

    Conditions such as inflation rates, interest rates, and employment levels, exert a profound influence on the dynamics of residential inventory supply & demand. During economic downturns, real estate demand may wane, whereas during periods of economic prosperity, it is likely to surge.

  4. Demographic Trends:

    Trivandrum’s real estate market is also shaped by demographic trends, including population growth, age distribution, and migration patterns. For instance, an ageing population may trigger heightened demand for specialised housing solutions such as retirement communities or assisted living facilities, reflecting the city’s evolving demographic landscape.

Conclusion for Best Places to Live in Trivandrum

Trivandrum is a city replete with diverse residential treasures, each offering its unique charm and amenities. While this article has shed light on highly sought-after residential areas, Akkulam emerges as the top choice. This tranquil lakeside haven perfectly blends nature’s serenity with urban convenience.

Whether you seek the serene ambiance of Mangalapuram, the bustling convenience of Kazhakkoottam, or the central location of Pattom, Trivandrum caters to a broad spectrum of lifestyles. Each neighbourhood has its unique character, and considering your specific needs will lead you to the area that accommodates your practical requirements and aligns with your dreams.

FAQs for Top Localities to Live in Trivandrum

1. Which is the posh area in Trivandrum?

Posh areas in Trivandrum are Kowdiar & Akkulam. Known for affluent residents, regal mansions, and proximity to leading institutions, Kowdiar & Akkulam exude sophistication and exclusivity.

2. Is Trivandrum an excellent place to settle?

Yes, Trivandrum is an excellent place to settle for many. It offers a unique blend of peaceful, green environment, excellent educational and healthcare facilities, and a growing job market in the IT sector. Trivandrum’s rich cultural heritage and relatively lower cost of living compared to other Indian cities make it an attractive destination for settling down.

3. Is Trivandrum cheap to live in?

Trivandrum is generally considered affordable compared to many major Indian cities. While living costs may vary, reasonable housing prices, accessible public transportation, and affordable dining options contribute to its reputation as a cost-effective living place.

4. What are the townships in Trivandrum?

Various townships in Trivandrum include the renowned Varkala taluk, Thiruvananthapuram taluk, Kattakada taluk, and Nedumanagad taluk.

5. Which is the best place in Trivandrum to buy a house?

Best places in Trivandrum to buy a house are Akkulam, Pattom, Kowdiar, and Vellayambalam. A few areas offer affordable housing, for example Karamana and Peroorkada.

6. Which city is the least expensive in Kerala?

Thiruvananthapuram is the least expensive city in Kerala. However, living costs vary in each city, depending on the specific area and lifestyle choices.

7. Is Trivandrum an excellent place to live?

Trivandrum is an excellent place to live due to its peaceful atmosphere, rich cultural heritage, excellent educational and healthcare facilities, and the growing job market. Its harmonious blend of tradition and modernity appeals to residents seeking a high quality of life.

8. Which is the wealthiest part of Kerala?

Wealthiest parts of Kerala are regions like Akkulam, Kowdiar, and Vellayambalam in Trivandrum and Panampilly Nagar in Kochi – as these are known for affluent residents and upscale properties.

9. Which are the low-lying areas in Trivandrum?

Low-lying areas in Trivandrum include parts of Vazhuthacaud, Manacaud, and Palayam. It is important to consider the risk of waterlogging when purchasing property in these regions.
