10 Sectors to Invest in Property in Gurgaon

December 30th, 2023

Gurgaon, or Gurugram, is one of the most prominent cities in India. Prime property investment sectors in Gurgaon include Sector 82, DLF City, Golf Course Extension, and South City. These areas offer diverse opportunities for residential and commercial ventures.

Known as the millennium city of India, Gurgaon’s real estate market has emerged as a thriving hub and has attracted investors with its dynamic landscape. In 1970, the city was established with the manufacturing plant of Maruti Suzuki India and BPO operations of General Electric, which played a significant part in the city’s development. While the city was popular for its agricultural trade, it has undergone a huge transformation in the 21st century to become one of the most preferred destinations for IT companies & automobile sectors.

As a burgeoning city in close proximity of Delhi, Gurgaon has gained substantial traction as a prime investment destination. The city is home to more than half of the Fortune 500 companies, including their regional centres and headquarters. The city’s rapid urbanisation and infrastructural development contribute to its growing significance among real estate enthusiasts.

Gurgaon has become a real estate hotspot due to its growing economy and excellent connectivity with Delhi-NCR via roads, Delhi Metro, and its own Rapid Metro. As per a few notable reports on the sales of residential properties, Delhi-NCR witnessed a growth of 65% YoY while Gurgaon solely contributed 33% of the share. The demand trend in this area shows that this number is set to rise in the upcoming years.

Latest Update

  • On January 12, 2023, the Municipal Corporation of Gurugram announced that the minimum requirement to register a plot in the city has now been reduced from 200 square yards to 100 square yards. According to the guidelines, a subdivided plot should not exceed 50 square yards.
  • As per the Haryana Management of Civic Amenities and Infrastructure Deficient Areas Outside Municipal Areas (Special Provisions) Act 2021, illegal colonies outside the Gurgaon area must apply for regularisation by January 18, 2023.
  • The Haryana government approved a policy to convert residential properties to commercial ones after paying the required amount. The policy is known as ‘Haryana Municipal Urban Built-Plan Reform’ and includes planned schemes.

Final Thoughts

Gurgaon’s real estate horizon beckons investors with a dynamic tapestry of opportunities across its top 10 sectors. From the strategic allure of Sector 112 to the harmonious blend of residential & commercial developments in Sector 88, each area presents a unique canvas for prospective investors.

Before making any property investment decisions, potential investors are encouraged to conduct detailed research and due diligence. Understanding each sector, ongoing development of property projects, and the potential for future growth is important for making informed choices.

With its strategic location, robust infrastructure, and thriving economic hubs, Gurgaon holds immense promise. The journey towards lucrative property investments begins with thorough research and an astute understanding of Gurgaon’s dynamic real estate opportunities.

FAQs

1. Which is the most promising sector to invest in property in Gurgaon?

The most promising sectors to invest in property in Gurgaon are Sectors 82 to 115. Strategic locations and significant growth potential make these an attractive choice for property investment. With rapid development of commercial & residential spaces, these areas offer a diversified portfolio for property investors looking for long-term gains.

2. Which is the best sector for rental income in Gurgaon?

The best sector for rental income in Gurgaon is Sector 47. Its proximity to employment hubs and well-developed infrastructure contribute to high demand for rental properties. Investors can capitalise on this demand, ensuring a steady and lucrative rental income stream.

3. Is it worth investing in a property in Gurgaon?

Yes, it is worth investing in a property in Gurgaon. The city's strategic location, robust infrastructure, and evolving real estate landscape offer ample opportunities for capital appreciation.

4. Where to invest in new Gurgaon?

You can invest in Sectors 88 & 112 in New Gurgaon. These sectors have planned development, strategic locations, and a perfect blend of residential & commercial properties, making them ideal for investors seeking growth potential in the evolving real estate market.

5. Which is the safest sector in Gurgaon?

Sector 56 is considered the safest sector in Gurgaon. With its established residential and commercial presence, along with a well-developed infrastructure, it offers a secure & stable environment for both residents and property investors.

6. Which is the wealthiest area in Gurgaon?

DLF Phase 2 is one of the wealthiest areas in Gurgaon. Home to upscale residential developments, commercial spaces, and high-end amenities, DLF Phase 2 represents the epitome of luxury living in the city.

7. What is the future of real estate in Gurgaon?

The future of real estate in Gurgaon is promising. The city continues to witness rapid urbanisation, infrastructural development, and increasing demand for commercial & residential spaces. With a focus on planned growth and connectivity improvements, Gurgaon is poised to remain a key player in the Indian real estate landscape. With a long-term perspective, buyers can benefit from the city's evolving real estate scenario.
