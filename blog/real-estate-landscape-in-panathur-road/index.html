For a homebuyer or a real estate investor, this area is a must-have in their investment portfolio. The Growing Appeal of Panathur Road

What makes Panathur Road one of the most popular real estate destinations in Bangalore – a city that is growing at an exponential rate? It is important for homebuyers or real estate investors to understand the transformation of Panathur Road from a small suburb to a real estate hotspot.

The IT boom earned Bangalore a place in the information technology landscape of the world. When multinational IT companies started their operations in Bangalore, there was a demand for huge office spaces, as infrastructure development is critical for business. The 60 km Outer Ring Road (ORR) was developed to increase connectivity of these office spaces with different parts of Bangalore. Today, ORR is the lifeline for lakhs of employees who work in large tech parks and from K R Puram to Silk Board.

With increased connectivity, areas that flank the ORR started gaining popularity as employees started preferring to stay in vicinity of their offices. Bellandur was one such destination. Today, it is home to some of the most prominent tech parks in Bangalore. Naturally, Panathur Road, which is easily accessible from the Outer Ring Road became a favourite destination for real estate developers, homebuyers, and investors.

In addition to its seamless connectivity, Panathur Road has a well-developed social infrastructure. With reputed schools, educational institutions, shopping centres, recreational facilities and more in the vicinity, the locale is ideal for those who prefer ease of living.

Current Real Estate Trends on Panathur Road

With demand for housing on Panathur Road steadily on the rise, major real estate players in Bangalore, including SOBHA Limited, are developing multiple projects in the locality. After the incredible success of SOBHA Dream Acres and SOBHA Sentosa, the much-awaited SOBHA Neopolis witnessed an unprecedented response during the pre-launch. In just two days, hundreds of Greek-themed luxury apartments were sold. This reiterated the ever-increasing demand for housing on Panathur Road.

A. Residential Property Trends on Panathur Road

In accordance with the current trend in real estate, apartments constitute the maximum number of houses on Panathur Road. Though villas and plotted developments are also available, they form a small segment.

91% of total residential units in the locality are apartments. Among them, the most popular configuration is 3BHK, which accounts for 43% of the overall sales that take place on Panathur Road. The average price per square foot is ₹9,906. The most common price range for properties in the area is ₹60 lakh – ₹1.2 crore.

B. Commercial Property Trends on Panathur Road

Just like it is a favourite destination for residential properties, Panathur Road also has a wide range of commercial properties – from retail establishments to corporate spaces. The growing demand for commercial properties in the locality is resulting in an increasing rental yield potential for the region. From an investment point of view, considering commercial properties on Panathur Road will be a wise decision as it holds the prospect for immense growth and appreciation.

C. Infrastructure and Connectivity

One of the most important factors that make Panathur Road a favourite destination for homebuyers and developers is its enhanced connectivity. Once the under-construction CDP (Comprehensive Development Plan) Road network and Metro Phase 2 are completed, Panathur Road will witness unparalleled connectivity. In turn, property prices in the area will further increase, owing to tremendous appreciation.

Factors to Consider Before Investing on Panathur Road

  • Budget and Financial Planning:

    Before you zero in on a project, be sure about your budget. If the project you have your eyes on comes within your budget, go ahead. This way, you can ensure that you don’t find yourself in a financial quagmire later.

  • Due Diligence and Property Inspection:

    Due diligence is of utmost importance when buying a property. If the property you are planning to buy is just launched and the work hasn’t commenced yet, make sure that you pay a site visit and see the mock-up apartment. If it is a ready-to-move in residence, visit the property and do a thorough check.

  • Legal Considerations & Documentation:

    Valid legal documents are crucial and without them, a property has no legal standing. Make sure that you check all the documents such as title deeds carefully with the help of a legal expert.

  • Risk Assessment and Exit Strategies:

    Consider all the liabilities and any unforeseen incidents in the future, before finalising your home. Assess your ability for long-term financing and work out a detailed plan to lessen your financial risks, in case a situation pops up wherein you will have to relinquish the property.

FAQs

1. Is Panathur urban or rural?

Panathur is an urban area in East Bangalore, and it is located near Marathahalli, Kadubeesanahalli, Bellandur, HSR Layout, Whitefield, and ITPL. Panathur is within commuting distance of major tech parks on Outer Ring Road.

2. Is Panathur a good place to live?

Panathur has it all. Yes, Panathur is a good place to live, with close proximity to Outer Ring Road and presence of all necessary conveniences in the vicinity. Panathur has seamless connectivity, good social infrastructure, and safety.

3. What are rural areas and urban areas?

Rural areas comprise of villages and small towns, while urban areas are metros and large cities. In the context of Bangalore city, the former fall under Bangalore rural district, and the latter are a part of Bangalore urban district.

4. What is Panathur famous for?

Panathur is famous for being an excellent residential area near large tech parks on ORR. Panathur is a favourite real estate destination in East Bangalore.

5. Which metro station is closest to Panathur, Bangalore?

Currently, Kundalahalli metro station is closest to Panathur, Bangalore. Once the Phase 2 Metro, which is under construction, gets completed, Kadubeesanahalli metro station will be closest to Panathur.

6. Is Panathur Bangalore north or south?

Panathur is in Bangalore East. It is a popular residential area adjacent to the Outer Ring Road. The locality is also close to Bellandur and Sarjapur Road.
