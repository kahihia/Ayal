    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the starting price of SOBHA Metropolis?

    The starting price of SOBHA Metropolis is INR 1.47 Crore* onwards.

    What is the total area size of SOBHA Metropolis?

    The total area size of SOBHA Metropolis is 8.41 acres.

    Where is SOBHA Metropolis located?

    SOBHA Metropolis is located at Ollur Road, Kuttanellur, Thrissur, Kerala 680014

    What are the key amenities at SOBHA Metropolis?

    The amenities at SOBHA Metropolis include 24 hrs Power Backup, Clubhouse, Gym, Tennis Court, Children’s Play Area, Cricket Pitch, Events Plaza, and more…

    What is the completion date of SOBHA Metropolis?

    Construction is in progress. It will be completed by December 2026.

    How many apartments are there in SOBHA Metropolis?

    SOBHA Metropolis has a total of 504 apartments.

    What type of apartments are there in SOBHA Metropolis?

    SOBHA Metropolis has 2, 3 & 4 BHK apartments.
