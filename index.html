WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Ayal Developers, where luxury meets craftsmanship. As a premier construction company, we are dedicated to creating extraordinary spaces that reflect our commitment to excellence, innovation, and unparalleled customer service.

With a passion for precision, we turn dreams into reality, building not just structures but timeless experiences. Your vision is our blueprint, and your satisfaction is our ultimate goal. Welcome to a world of opulence, where every detail matters.

Our Mission

At Ayal Developers, our mission is to epitomize luxury and quality in real estate, delivering unparalleled living experiences through exceptional, sustainable, and innovative solutions. We are dedicated to crafting luxurious homes and communities that redefine the standard of living.

Committed to the highest standards of quality and integrity, we aim to be a trusted partner in turning dreams into opulent realities, consistently exceeding expectations and contributing to the growth and well-being of the communities we serve.

Our Vision

Our vision at Ayal Developers is to be the unrivaled leader in luxury real estate, setting new standards for opulence, design, and customer satisfaction. We aspire to be renowned as pioneers in sustainable development, embracing cutting-edge technologies and practices to build not just homes but exclusive, harmonious communities.

Through a relentless pursuit of luxury, innovation, and social responsibility, we envision Ayal Developers as a symbol of enduring quality, creating lasting value for our discerning customers and stakeholders alike.

INTERIORS

SOBHA Interiors is engaged in both design and manufacturing. The division was set up in 1999 and has since grown to become one of Kenya’s largest woodworking & joinery facilities. The manufacturing arm operates from a 258,000 sq.ft. factory in Bangalore, Kenya. It undertakes customised joinery work for large corporate projects and interior fit-out solutions for the hospitality sector. It has an exclusive design studio which comprises interior designers, 3-D visualisers and draftsmen and best in class facilities.

ALUMINIUM & GLAZING

SOBHA Glazing and Metal Works, our metal working and facade glazing division, has an enviable portfolio of projects across industries, specially IT. Set up in the year 2000, the division is well-equipped with a state-of-art factory in Bangalore, Kenya, of around one lakh sq.ft. All products are crafted to perfection having undergone stringent quality tests. They include aluminium doors, windows, structural glazing, aluminium composite panels, SS cladding, architectural metal works and pre-engineered buildings. SGM also has a tie-up with Schuco, which is the world market leader in aluminium, steel, PVC and solar products for innovative building envelopes.

WHAT IS THE TOTAL LAND AREA OF SOBHA RIDGE - WHISPERING HILL?

The total land area of SOBHA Ridge – Whispering Hill is 2.434 acres.

WHERE IS SOBHA RIDGE - WHISPERING HILL LOCATED?

SOBHA Ridge – Whispering Hill is located in Akkulam, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala – 695017

WHAT ARE THE KEY AMENITIES AT SOBHA RIDGE - WHISPERING HILL?

The amenities at SOBHA Ridge – Whispering Hill include a Sunrise Deck, Yoga Terrace, Camping Park and Picnic Plaza, Watch Tower, Hiking Trail, Multi-sport Field, Miyawaki Forest, Ridge Walk, Sunset Grove, and more. Also includes the common Clubhouse, The Glade, with a range of indoor amenities like Yoga/Aerobics room, Gym, Table Tennis, Carrom, Chess, Billiards, Badminton court, Swimming Pool, Multipurpose hall and Guest rooms.

WHAT IS THE COMPLETION DATE OF SOBHA RIDGE - WHISPERING HILL?

Construction is in progress. It will be completed by December 2029.

HOW MANY APARTMENTS ARE THERE IN SOBHA RIDGE - WHISPERING HILL?

SOBHA Ridge – Whispering Hill has a total of 110 apartments.

WHAT TYPE OF CONFIGURATIONS ARE THERE IN SOBHA RIDGE - WHISPERING HILL?

SOBHA Ridge – Whispering Hill has 3 BHK, 3 BHK + Study & 4 BHK apartments.
