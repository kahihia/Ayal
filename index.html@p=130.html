INTERIORS

SOBHA Interiors is engaged in both design and manufacturing. The division was set up in 1999 and has since grown to become one of India’s largest woodworking & joinery facilities. The manufacturing arm operates from a 258,000 sq.ft. factory in Bangalore, India. It undertakes customised joinery work for large corporate projects and interior fit-out solutions for the hospitality sector. It has an exclusive design studio which comprises interior designers, 3-D visualisers and draftsmen and best in class facilities.

ALUMINIUM & GLAZING

SOBHA Glazing and Metal Works, our metal working and facade glazing division, has an enviable portfolio of projects across industries, specially IT. Set up in the year 2000, the division is well-equipped with a state-of-art factory in Bangalore, India, of around one lakh sq.ft. All products are crafted to perfection having undergone stringent quality tests. They include aluminium doors, windows, structural glazing, aluminium composite panels, SS cladding, architectural metal works and pre-engineered buildings. SGM also has a tie-up with Schuco, which is the world market leader in aluminium, steel, PVC and solar products for innovative building envelopes.

TRAINING CENTRES

Infosys Global Education Center II is the largest monolithic structure to be constructed in Independent India. Designed by architect Hafeez Contractor and built by SOBHA , it has a built-up area of 1,002,095 sq. ft. It has eighty four 100-seater, six 60-seater, three 200-seater, and two 36-seater class rooms, five examination halls, 19 meeting rooms, and 302 faculty cabins. It also has an induction hall, a state-of-the-art library, a food court, a cyber cafe and a 40 seat Management Council room. It’s indeed a masterpiece of Modern India!
