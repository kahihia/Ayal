Connectivity

The 9-kilometre NICE Road connects Bangalore and Mysore, making commuting convenient. Transport to locations like Tumkur, Magadi, Mysore, Kanakapura, Bannerghatta, and Hosur Road is made simpler through this stretch. Along with NICE Road, the 10-kilometre elevated Hosur Road links Madiwala and Electronic City.

The Outer Ring Road also provides easy access to the airport from East and North Bangalore. However, if you prefer not to drive, go with the excellent Namma metro service.

Tips for Property Investment in Bangalore South

– Market Research and Analysis Before Investing

It would be best if you always did a real estate market study whether buying or selling a flat. It will help you understand the current market, how much comparable properties are worth, if it is an investment property, how much you can charge for rent, etc. By being aware of housing market pricing, you may always bargain for a lower price and prevent paying more than the house is worth.

– Evaluating the Potential of Specific Areas

When you are looking for a property, consider the local amenities. Buyers typically seek out convenient stores, food outlets, and entertainment. Consider how far away the highway, workplaces, malls, etc., from your home are. The value of an apartment will often increase with proximity to amenities. Of course, what constitutes a desirable location may vary from person to person, but other objective elements affect a home’s worth.

– Financial Planning and Home Loan Options

Financial preparation is essential before homeowners begin their journey, since a luxury apartment is the largest purchase one makes in life. Having enough money for the down payment – generally around 20% of the price – is sometimes the most significant obstacle to purchasing a house. Therefore, save for a down payment well in advance, if you intend to buy a home. Aim to save at least 25% of the buying price, and remember to consider inflation when figuring this amount.
